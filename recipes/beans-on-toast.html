Beans on Toast

Description

Definitely a recipe for beans on toast and not just lasagna again because I was too lazy to change it!

My secret is mozzarella on top! Optional side dishes include a salad, garlic bread or my personal favourite, more lasagna.

Ingrediants

  • Pasta sheets
  • Mince meat
  • Onions
  • Carrots
  • Tinned tomatoes
  • Paprika
  • Garlic
  • Chedder cheese
  • White sauce
  • Mozzarella

Steps

  1. Chop up your onions, begin to caramalise on a medium heat with a bit of oil
  2. Throw in your minced meat. Cut up your carrots pretty small and dash that in too
  3. Stick in some paprika and garlic and any other herbs you might want. Mix it all up
  4. Once onions have caramalised, mince browned and carrots softened then pour in your tinned tommys
  5. Pre-heat that bitch. Lets say 180
  6. Make your white sauce, milk, flour, butter... Google it
  7. Scoop out a layer of your mince, then cover with a layer of pasta sheets, on top of that stick some of your white sauce
  8. If you're a fatty boom batty cheese fiend like me, then chuck some chedder on each layer too
  9. Repeat that layering pattern until you've run out of mince
  10. To crown this beauty cut up some mozzarella on top and whack it in the oven
  11. Check it after 45 mins-ish, if cooked then you're free to enjoy that big cheesy bastard
